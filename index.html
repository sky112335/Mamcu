About MAMCU

Our Mission

At Masasi Mtwara Cooperative Union (MAMCU), our mission is to empower local farmers by providing reliable markets, ensuring fair and transparent payments, and offering extensive training and cooperative support.

Founded by and for farmers, MAMCU works through Agricultural Marketing Co-operative Societies (AMCOS) to collect and market high-quality cashew nuts, sesame seeds, and pigeon peas that meet international standards.

MAMCU Secures New Export Deal for Cashew Nuts

Published on June 10, 2025

MAMCU has successfully negotiated a new export agreement that will benefit over 5,000 local farmers in the Masasi and Mtwara regions. The deal, valued at several million dollars, represents a significant milestone in our mission to expand market access for our member farmers.

The agreement includes provisions for premium pricing, reflecting the high quality of our cashew nuts and sustainable farming practices. This new partnership will see MAMCU supplying premium grade cashews to major markets in Europe and Asia.

Key highlights of the deal include:

  • Guaranteed minimum purchase volumes for the next three seasons
  • Price premiums for certified organic production
  • Technical support for quality improvement
  • Investment in local processing facilities

This achievement reinforces MAMCU's position as a leading agricultural cooperative in Tanzania and demonstrates our commitment to creating sustainable value for our farming communities.

Farmer Training Program Expands to New Districts

Published on May 25, 2025

Following the success of our pilot training program, MAMCU is expanding its agricultural training initiative to reach farmers in five additional districts. This expansion will enable us to share modern farming techniques and sustainable practices with thousands more farmers.

The expanded program will focus on:

  • Sustainable farming practices and organic certification
  • Post-harvest handling and quality management
  • Climate-smart agriculture techniques
  • Financial literacy and farm management

The training program has already shown remarkable results, with participating farmers reporting yield increases of up to 40% and significant improvements in crop quality. This expansion represents MAMCU's ongoing commitment to empowering farmers with knowledge and skills for sustainable agriculture.

The program will be implemented in partnership with local agricultural extension officers and international development partners, ensuring comprehensive support for participating farmers.

New Storage Facility Inaugurated in Masasi

Published on April 15, 2025

MAMCU has opened a new state-of-the-art storage facility in Masasi district, enhancing our capacity to store and process agricultural products. This modern facility represents a significant investment in our infrastructure and will play a crucial role in maintaining product quality.

The new facility features:

  • Climate-controlled storage areas with 5000-ton capacity
  • Modern quality testing laboratory
  • Automated sorting and grading equipment
  • Sustainable energy systems including solar power

This facility will help reduce post-harvest losses and ensure consistent quality of our products throughout the year. It includes advanced humidity control systems and pest management features, setting new standards for agricultural storage in the region.

The inauguration ceremony was attended by local government officials, farmer representatives, and international partners, highlighting the importance of this investment for the region's agricultural development.

MAMCU Premium Cashew Nuts

Masasi regions. With decades of experience in cashew production, collection, and export, MAMCU ensures that every batch of nuts meets international standards for quality and freshness. The cooperative manages the entire process from farm-level harvesting to sorting, grading, and packaging ensuring transparency and consistency for every buyer.

MAMCU cashew nuts are known for their rich flavor, excellent kernel yield, and naturally clean processing. The union works closely with farmers to promote good agricultural practices, ensuring that the nuts are organically grown and sun-dried to preserve their natural taste and quality. Buyers can expect uniform sizes, well-dried shells, and minimal moisture content ideal for both roasting and industrial processing. The nuts are carefully inspected and graded before export, making them suitable for a wide range of international markets, including food processors, wholesalers, and retailers.

For new buyers, working with MAMCU LTD offers not only a reliable supply of premium cashew nuts but also a partnership rooted in trust, professionalism, and sustainability. The cooperative operates with full government registration and certification, guaranteeing legal and ethical trade. MAMCU provides flexible purchasing options, clear documentation, and logistical support for both domestic and export orders. By sourcing cashew nuts from MAMCU, buyers contribute to empowering local farmers, supporting community development, and strengthening Tanzania's agricultural economy.

Quality control is at the heart of MAMCU's cashew processing operations. Our state-of-the-art facilities employ rigorous testing procedures and modern equipment to ensure each batch meets the highest standards. From moisture content analysis to size grading and quality checks, every step is carefully monitored and documented. This dedication to quality has earned MAMCU a reputation for excellence in the global cashew market, making us a preferred supplier for discerning buyers worldwide.

MAMCU Premium Sesame Seeds

Masasi Mtwara Cooperative Union (MAMCU LTD) is one of the leading suppliers of high-quality sesame seeds in southern Tanzania. The union works directly with thousands of smallholder farmers who cultivate sesame using natural and sustainable farming practices. MAMCU LTD collects, processes, and markets sesame seeds from its member cooperative societies, ensuring that buyers receive clean, well-dried, and uniformly graded products. With years of experience in crop aggregation and export, the union guarantees consistency, transparency, and reliability in every delivery.

The sesame seeds from MAMCU LTD are known for their high oil content, purity, and excellent flavor, making them suitable for both industrial processing and direct consumption. The union follows strict quality control measures during harvesting, cleaning, and packaging to maintain international standards. Buyers can request both hulled and unhulled sesame seeds, depending on their needs. MAMCU LTD also provides full traceability from farm to market, giving customers confidence in the origin and integrity of the product.

For new buyers, partnering with MAMCU LTD means working with a trusted and farmer-centered organization that values long-term relationships. The union offers flexible supply arrangements, competitive pricing, and efficient logistics for local and international orders. By sourcing sesame seeds from MAMCU LTD, buyers not only gain access to premium-quality products but also support thousands of small-scale farmers who depend on sesame cultivation for their livelihoods. MAMCU LTD remains committed to integrity, quality, and mutual growth with all its trading partners.

MAMCU Premium Pigeon Peas

Masasi Mtwara Cooperative Union (MAMCU LTD) is one of Tanzania's trusted suppliers of high-quality pigeon peas, produced by its network of dedicated smallholder farmers across the southern regions. The cooperative ensures that all pigeon peas are grown under natural conditions, without harmful chemicals, resulting in clean, nutritious, and export-standard grains. With years of experience in crop aggregation and export logistics, MAMCU LTD provides buyers with reliable, large-scale supply while maintaining consistent quality and timely delivery.

MAMCU LTD's pigeon peas are carefully sorted, graded, and processed to meet both local and international market requirements. The union follows strict post-harvest handling proceduressuch as proper drying, cleaning, and packagingto preserve the natural color, flavor, and shelf life of the crop. Buyers can expect uniform grain size, low moisture content, and minimal impurities, making MAMCU pigeon peas ideal for food processing, wholesale trading, or direct consumption. The cooperative also offers flexible packaging and bulk supply options to meet the specific needs of different buyers.

By purchasing pigeon peas from MAMCU LTD, buyers not only receive top-quality produce but also support a sustainable farming system that empowers thousands of rural farmers. The cooperative reinvests profits into improving farmer training, infrastructure, and modern farming techniques to increase productivity and maintain consistent supply throughout the year. Partnering with MAMCU LTD guarantees not only premium-quality pigeon peas but also a transparent, ethical, and long-term business relationship built on trust, traceability, and shared growth.

MAMCU Premium Green Gram

Masasi Mtwara Cooperative Union (MAMCU LTD) is proud to introduce Green Gram (mung beans) as one of its newly added agricultural crops for trade. Known for its rich nutritional value and growing demand both locally and internationally, Green Gram represents an exciting new opportunity for buyers and farmers alike. MAMCU LTD, with its long-standing experience in managing and marketing crops such as cashew nuts and sesame, now extends its expertise to ensure the production and supply of high-quality Green Gram from the fertile lands of southern Tanzania.

This new crop is cultivated by MAMCU member farmers under close supervision to guarantee consistency, purity, and quality. The Green Gram is carefully harvested, cleaned, and graded using modern techniques to meet export and domestic standards. Buyers can expect well-dried, naturally grown mung beans with high protein content and excellent cooking quality. MAMCU ensures traceability from farm to market, providing transparency and confidence for new partners interested in sourcing premium Tanzanian Green Gram.

As this is a newly introduced crop under MAMCU LTD, the union welcomes new buyers and trading partners to join hands in building a sustainable and mutually beneficial market network. With established logistics systems, reliable farmer networks, and a commitment to fair trade, MAMCU guarantees both quality supply and professional service. By purchasing Green Gram from MAMCU LTD, buyers not only gain access to top-quality produce but also contribute to the empowerment of local farmers and the growth of Tanzania's agricultural economy.
